Cursor of Power is a lightweight World of Warcraft addon that enhances cursor visibility with a clean arcane ring and optional sparkle trail. 
Designed for players who want their cursor to stand out during raids, Mythic+, PvP, or general gameplay without adding UI clutter.




Arcane Cursor Ring

A smooth, glowing ring that follows your cursor in real time, making it easy to track during combat or fast movement.

Optional Sparkle Trail

  - Adds magical arcane sparkles that fade out naturally as you move the cursor.
  - posibility to change color based on enemy, neutral, or friendly targets.



  -----------------------------------------------
  How to install manualy.

    1 Locate your World of warcraft game folder
    2 Copy the CursorOfPower folder to  <path to your wow folder>\World of Warcraft\_retail_\Interface\AddOns
    3 restart the game or type /reload in chat if the game is already running.


CursorOfPower_Social  is a plugin for the core addon that adds visibility for friends and guildmates.
A separate ring will show to visualise people are in your guild or on your friend list.
